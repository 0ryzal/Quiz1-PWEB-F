Sidoarjo Culinary

Discover the most popular destinations in Sidoarjo

Lontong Kupang

Lontong Kupang is a lontong that is sliced mixed with garlic, chili and a little petis which is mashed until evenly distributed, then doused with boiled mussels and lento slices.

4.7

Bandeng Presto

All the bones in it can also be eaten, because milkfish bones feel soft like Flesh. The taste produced from this culinary, which is savory with tender meat and soft to the bone.

4.5

Otak-otak Bandeng

Milkfish meat is processed by mashing and mixing various typical spices, such as coconut Grated roast, coconut milk, sugar and salt to taste, and other spices.

4.8

Bandeng Asap

Still using the basic ingredient of milkfish, this time it is known as Smoked Milkfish. Technique Processing this food is by using hot smoke until it is cooked evenly.

4.8

Ote-ote Porong

This culinary uses ingredients similar to fried bakwan, namely from flour dough mixed oysters and black seaweed, as well as a selection of spices.

4.8

Sate Kerang

This animal that lives on the seabed is processed by cleaning first, then boiling until cooked. This typical Sidoarjo food has a savory, slightly sweet, and spicy taste.

4.8
